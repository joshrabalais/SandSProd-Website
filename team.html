The Team of
Short and Stout Productions

Josh Rabalais, Director/Writer/Executive Producer:

Josh Rabalais is a lifelong film fan, dedicated to delivering the films he longed to see more of as a child. He directed his first film in high school, and from that a passion was born. To this day, he writes films that aim to entertain and look at the world in a much different way.

Having made a name for himself, he now leads Short and Stout Productions with his wonderful wife Sydney Knight-Lagrone to take the filmmaking world by storm with new and unique stories!


Sydney Knight-Lagrone, Executive Producer/Editor:

Sydney Knight-Lagrone has been performing from a young age. From using hairspray as smoke for her family concerts, or being the life of the party, Sydney has done it all.

Supporting her husband's dream, she produces and edits the various projects that Short and Stout Productions finds themselves producing.

Syl V., Executive Producer:

Syl V. (DOB unknown) is a two-time consecutive MBA dropout who does businessy things and serves (against company wishes) as the studio's Meme Queen.

In her free time, she enjoys arguing on the Internet.

Jake Williams, Cinematographer:

Jake Williams is a film enthusiast, and photography expert! Doing almost any kind of photography imaginable, he specializes in his films sharing a unique look.

His photography is nothing short of breathtaking, and he brings each Short and Stout production to life.
